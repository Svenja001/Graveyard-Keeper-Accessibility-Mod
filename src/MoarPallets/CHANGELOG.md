# Changelog

## 0.1.0 | 16 May 2026

- Adds the pallet to the wooden build desk and the cellar build desk, costing 6 flitch and 4 nails.
- Pallets can be removed via the build desk's Remove button, returning half the build cost.
- The cellar elevator and barn porter now reach pallets anywhere in the cellar, not just next to the elevator.
- Pallets placed in the barn add merchant trading capacity. Anywhere else they work as extra single-slot containers.
